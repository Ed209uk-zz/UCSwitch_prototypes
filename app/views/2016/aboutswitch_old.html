You’ll need to switch to Universal Credit in March 2018

What you need to do to get ready

  • Make sure you can get access to the internet, either at home or at a local library or friend’s house - Universal Credit is all done online. If you're not that confident using a computer or the internet, contact your local library to find out about courses to help improve your skills
  • Set up an email account if you don’t have one already
  • Set up a bank or building society account to make payments from. If you have a partner, you might want to set up a joint account with them as you’ll get 1 monthly payment between the two of you
  • When you're working out your bills don’t forget your rent, including if you’re renting your home off a housing association that’s paid for by housing benefit.
  • If you’d like extra help working out your budgets go to [online budgeting tool]
  • Get yourself verified using GOV.UK/Verify. This will save you having to go to the job centre with your identification.
